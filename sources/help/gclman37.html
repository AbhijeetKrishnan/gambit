AddStrategy

AddStrategy[support->NFSUPPORT, strategy->STRATEGY] =: NFSUPPORT
Adds strategy to the support. If the strategy is already in the support, no change is made. A copy of the modified support is returned. It is an error if support and strategy are not from the same normal form game.

See also: RemoveStrategy (REF NOT FOUND), Support (REF NOT FOUND).
