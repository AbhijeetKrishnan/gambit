In the extensive form, the game is represented as a topological tree. The section on representation of the Extensive Form has a more detailed explanation of this form. Compared to the Normal Form, the Extensive Form interface is much richer and thus considerably more complex. A major portion of the functionality is devoted to the tree building. Another set of functions deals with customizing the display, and yet another set of functions takes care of the solutions and their display.
