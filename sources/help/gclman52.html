AllPerfect

AllPerfect[n->NFG, {stopAfter->0}, {nTries->0}, 
           {precision->Float}] =: LIST(MIXED)
Searches for all Perfect Nash equilibria of the normal form game n. Returns a list containing the equilibria found.
