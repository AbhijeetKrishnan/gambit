The principal developers of Gambit are:
  • Richard D. McKelvey
  • Andrew McLennan
  • Theodore Turocy
The development of the core functionality took place in 1994-1996, under a grant from the National Science Foundation to the California Institute of Technology and the University of Minnesota (McKelvey and McLennan, principal investigators), which we gratefully acknolwedge.

The development of Gambit has been aided in the past by:

  • Eugene Grayver: development of the first version of the graphical user interface frontend
  • Gary Wu: extensive implementation work on the command language
  • Bernhard von Stengel: Provided advice on implementation of sequence form code, and contributed clique code

Many other students at Caltech and University of Minnesota have helped over the years with the project.
